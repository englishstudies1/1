Our Story

English Studies at FLSHM

Welcome to the English Studies Department at the Faculty of Letters and Humanities. This portal centralises all academic resources for our students.

Our department is committed to providing high-quality education in linguistics, literature, and language skills across GR1 and GR2.

This platform is your one-stop hub: access your weekly schedule, study materials, makeup sessions, and stay connected with your fellow students.
